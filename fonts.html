<폰트 3개 소개>

  1. Song Myung

    - Song Myung is a Korean and Latin font that is a contemporary take on typical Hangul serif fonts,
    less ornaments and larger consonants. The Latin is a version of Source Serif Pro adjusted to fit with the Korean design.
    - link 방식으로 폰트 로딩

  2. Gasoek One

    - When people mention "Soek" in South Korea, they will think of the Chinese character.
    This word gives the impression of being as thick and hard as a stone. Ga means "to add" in Korean.
    So this typeface, Gasoek, is a much thicker typeface than usual typefaces.

  3. Cute Font

    - Cute Font is a Korean and Latin font that features round joints and
    terminals while emphasizing the contrast of horizontal and vertical strokes.
    - 다운로드방식으로 폰트로딩
